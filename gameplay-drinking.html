Getting a Drink

There are very few ways to get drinks in The Forest, these are as follows:

Method Details
Collecting There are multiple ways to collect water. The main way is through using your hands, however this will result in drinking polluted water. You can also get polluted water if you own a waterskin or an old pot. Finally, there is the ability to build a water collector. Water collectors will fill up naturally with clean water when it rains. For the recipe of the water collector, see the survival guide. For guidance on how to get the resources, see the resources page. For guidance on how to build, select the 'building' element under 'building elements' to the left of your screen. You may also use the quick navigation options below.
Foraging This method requires a crafted item called a 'pouch'. Once you have crafted the 'pouch', you need to equip it. You'll then want to find a bush that bears berries and you'll have the option to collect them.
Exploration For this method, all you have to do is explore. You'll come across soda cans throughout the map and you'll also be able to have the chance of getting them through breaking open suitcases.

Boiling Water

To boil water, you must first have collected water in an old pot, you may then boil it over a campfire. This will turn the water from polluted to clean. For the recipe of the campfire, see the survival guide. For guidance on how to get the resources, see the resources page. For guidance on how to build, select the 'building' element under 'building elements' to the left of your screen. You may also use the quick navigation options below.

Drinking

To drink a soda, you'll need to access your inventory and from there you'll be able to hover over the soda you wish to drink and it will give you the option to drink it. To drink from a waterskin, you may do the same and access it from your inventory. To drink from an old pot, you can fill it up or boil it on a campfire, then you make drink it in the same way, except when you boil it, you will be able to drink it straight from the campfire by looking at it. Finally, you may drink from a water collector by looking at it when there is water in it. For the recipe of the water collector or campfire, see the survival guide. For guidance on how to get the resources, see the resources page. For guidance on how to build, select the 'building' element under 'building elements' to the left of your screen. Water Quality

All water has a quality. This means that the water can be clean or polluted. The various qualities are as follows:

Quality Details
Clean This kind of water is considered safe, you make drink it till your heart is content.
Polluted This kind of water can poison the player and should not be drank unless you need it for survival.
