Introductory Storyline

The Forest is a survival based game where players first start out in an airplane with their son. The airplane then crashes and the cutscene cuts out. Upon re-opening of the cutscene, you see a strange human-like, cannibalistic mutant approach and take your son. With you weakened from the plane crash, you are unable to prevent this. Once the cutscene then ends, it's the players task to survive, upgrade their gear and explore the environment in order to locate their son.
