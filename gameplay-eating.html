Getting Food

There are a couple of ways to get food in The Forest, these are as follows:

Method Details
Hunting This method is done by killing the wildlife, you must kill an animal then you may skin the animal to remove the skin. From there, you'll be able to collect the animals meat.
Fishing There is no direct fishing in the game, to get fish you'll need to directly kill the fish swimming around in the pond. Once killed, you'll be able to pick the fish up.
Foraging This method requires a crafted item called a 'pouch'. Once you have crafted the 'pouch', you need to equip it. You'll then want to find a bush that bears berries and you'll have the option to collect them.
Exploration For this method, all you have to do is explore. You'll come across snack bars throughout the map and you'll also be able to have the chance of getting them through breaking open suitcases.
Cannibalism This method, despite it's unhumane nature, is a method you may use to get food. For this method, you'll need to find and kill a cannabal. From there, you'll need to chop the body up into pieces. Once done, you'll be able to collect the various body parts to obtain the edible pieces.

Cooking Food

Cooking food first requires you to build a campfire. To cook an item, you will have to look at a lit campfire and it will provide the option to add the food you are holding in your inventory. Alternatively, there is the abilty to equip specific food and then place it on the campfire by looking at it. For the recipe of the campfire, see the survival guide. Drying Food

This method requires a buildable structure called a 'drying rack'. Once you have built the 'drying rack', you will then want to place your meat on it. To do this, you will have to look at the drying rack and it will provide the option to add the food you are holding in your inventory. Alternatively, there is the abilty to equip specific food and then place it on the drying rack by looking at it. For the recipe of the drying rack, see the survival guide. Eating Food

To eat food, whether this is raw, cooked or dried, you'll need to access your inventory and from there you'll be able to hover over the item you wish to eat and it will give you the option to eat it.

Food Quality

All food, including limbs, has a quality of life. This means that the food can spoil. The various qualities are as follows:

Quality Details
Fresh If the food is fresh, there is no effect when consuming it. The only exception is limbs, eating these raw will still make your character sick.
Edible If the food is considered edible, there is no effect when consuming it. The only exception is limbs, eating these in an 'edible' state will still make your character sick.
Spoilt If the food is considered spoilt, your character has 100% chance to gain sickness (you can view your effects in the survival guide).
State Details
Raw If the food is raw, there may be a chance of sickness (needs testing). Raw food will also spoil.
Cooked If the food is cooked, there is no chance of sickness. Cooked food will also spoil.
Dried If the food is dried, there is no chance of sickness. Dried food will not spoil, but will reduce hydration.
