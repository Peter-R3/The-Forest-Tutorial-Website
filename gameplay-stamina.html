What drains stamina

Stamina will drain naturally as you do different activities around the world, these are as follows:

Activity Details
Swinging a weapon Swinging a weapon around, whether you are swinging at a cannabal or swinging for the sake of swinging, will drain your stamina.
Sprinting Stamina will drain naturally as you sprint around.
Sprint Swimming Stamina will drain naturally if you 'spring swim' or swim faster. A 'sprint swim' is holding sprint in water in order to swim faster.
Cutting down trees Chopping trees will reduce your stamina over time, much like swinging a weapon.
Climbing a rope Climbing a rope will quickly drain your stamina if you stay moving on it for too long (you can recover stamina by staying still on the rope).

What doesn't drain stamina

Stamina will not drain naturally if you do any of the following different activities:

Activity Details
Walking Walking (not sprinting) will not drain your stamina.
Doing nothing If you do absolutely nothing, surprisingly this won't drain your stamina. Shocking!
Swimming (not sprint swimming) Swimming (not sprint swimming) will not drain your stamina.
Using the repair tool Using the repair tool to fix buildable structures will not drain your stamina.
Ground attacks with spears Hitting the ground (specifically the ground) with a crafted spear, will not drain your stamina.
Rowing a boat Rowing a boat will not drain stamina.
Descending a rope Descending (goind down, not climbing) will not drain your stamina.
