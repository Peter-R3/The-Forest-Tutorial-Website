Getting armour

Armour acts as an additional protective layer to the player when taking damage. It protects not only damage from the player being attacked, but also from diffrentiating climate temperatures. Armour can be obtained by hunting or crafting.

Types of armour

There are several types of armour with different properites, each with various pieces. They are as follows:
